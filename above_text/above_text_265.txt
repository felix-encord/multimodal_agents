Change collaborator role 

To change the role of a collaborator on the Ontology, click the dropdown and select the role you want to apply to the user.

- Users with the _Admin_ role can modify the Ontology
- Users with the _Viewer_ role cannot make changes to the Ontology. However, a _Viewer_ can copy the Ontology.

<Note>An _Admin_ cannot be reverted to a _Viewer_. To revoke Admin access for a user, contact support@encord.com.</Note>
