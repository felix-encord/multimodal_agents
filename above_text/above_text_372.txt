Annotator submissions

<Tip>Click the **+** button next to a user's email address to reveal the Annotator submission details. </Tip>

The _Annotator submissions_ tab lists the following information:

- **Annotator**: The user's email address.
- **Data Unit**: The task's file name.
- **Instance**: The ontology instance being referred to.
