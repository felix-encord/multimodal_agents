User permissions for Folders are configured by selecting a Folder in **Files**. Click **Manage access** after selecting a Folder to add or remove users from the Folder. 

<Note>Only users that are a part of your Organization can be added to your Folders.</Note>

Users added to your Folders have access to all files in the Folder.

<center>

| Permission      | Admin | Viewer |
|-----------------|-------|--------|
| View Folder     | ✅     | ✅     |
| Add data        | ✅     | ❌     |
| Adjust settings | ✅     | ❌     |

</center>
