Re-encode from Folders

<Info>Re-encoding is only available to Enterprise customers. Contact sales@encord.com for more information.</Info>

1. Go to **Index > Files**.
   A list of all _Folders_ available appears on the _Files_ page.

2. Go into the Folder where the video you want to re-encode resides.

3. Perform one of the following:

  - Right-click the video and select **Re-encode** and select the type of re-encoding to perform.
