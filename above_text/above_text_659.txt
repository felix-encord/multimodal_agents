Re-encode from Datasets

<Info>Re-encoding is only available to Enterprise customers. Contact sales@encord.com for more information.</Info>

1. Go to **Index > Datasets**.
   A list of all Datasets available appears on the _Datasets_ page.

2. Go into the Dataset where the video you want to re-encode resides.

3. Select one or more videos and 

4. Click **Re-encode** and select the type of re-encoding to perform.
