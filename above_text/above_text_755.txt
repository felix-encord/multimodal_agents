Apply Classifications to Collections

<AccordionGroup>
   <Accordion title="To apply Classifications to Collections and send them to Annotate:">

1. Import your Project that has the Ontology with Classifications.

2. Click the Project once import completes.
   The Project opens with the Explorer page displaying.
   
3. Filter the Project data in _Data_, _Labels_, or _Predictions_ in the Explorer by searching, sorting, and filtering until you have the subset of the data you need.
