Encord _Annotate_ allows you to efficiently label visual data, manage large-scale annotation teams, and ensure high-quality data for machine learning applications through customizable workflows and quality control tools. [Get started with _Annotate_](/platform-documentation/GettingStarted/annotate-getting-started/annotate-register-aws-data) to streamline your labeling processes and produce top-tier training data.

***

## When to use Encord Annotate?

Encord Annotate is designed to support your machine learning pipeline by providing tools to efficiently turn unlabeled data into labeled data that can be used to train models.

![Encord Annotate diagram](https://storage.googleapis.com/docs-media.encord.com/static/img/process-chart-ea.webp)


***

## What data does Encord Annotate support?

Annotate supports various data formats and modalities, providing the tools to efficiently label large Datasets. It is optimized for high performance, even with extensive datasets and complex labeling tasks.
